ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Horizon Mechanics LLC-FZ emerges as a trusted partner in the aviation supply chain, rooted in the strategic hub of MeydanFree Zone, Dubai, UAE.


Our expertise covers the sales of spare parts, AOG, exchange, repair and maintenance, Diagnostics and sourcing services, aligning with our mission to connect and establish enduring relationships with leading aviation OEM manufacturers and suppliers worldwide.


Our service initiatives prioritize consistent and dynamic interaction, aiming to fully comprehend our clients' unique needs. Through mutual involvement, we pursue continuous improvement to reach common goals and objectives.


Horizon Mechanics LLC-FZ is persistent in its commitment to quality and service, preferring a personalized approach to satisfy each client's unique needs. This personalized strategy ensures the highest level of performance, efficiency, and reliability in our products and services. We are dedicated to exploring innovative opportunities that enhance our service offering and industry presence.
