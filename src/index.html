DRIVING CONFIDENCE, DELIVERING EXCELLENCE

ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Based in the strategic hub of Meydan Free Zone, Dubai, UAE, Horizon Mechanics LLC-FZ is your trusted partner in the aviation supply chain.


With expertise spanning spare parts sales, AOG support, exchanges, repairs and maintenance, diagnostics, and sourcing services, we are committed to connecting with and establishing enduring relationships with leading aviation OEM manufacturers and suppliers worldwide.


At Horizon Mechanics LLC-FZ, our service initiatives prioritize consistent and dynamic interaction with our clients. We aim to fully comprehend their unique needs and collaborate closely to achieve common goals and objectives through continuous improvement.


Committed to quality and service excellence, we adopt a personalized approach to meet the distinctive requirements of each client. This tailored strategy ensures the highest level of performance, efficiency, and reliability in our products and services.


Driven by innovation, we are dedicated to exploring new opportunities that enhance our service offerings and strengthen our presence in the industry.


Thank you for choosing Horizon Mechanics LLC-FZ as your aviation partner. We look forward to serving you with excellence.

SERVICES

Committed to Excellence: Enhancing Your Experience Every Step of the Way.

Spare Parts

Horizon Mechanics's mission is to offer the purchase, storage, and sale of all civil aviation products and parts required, ensuring seamless supply chain management and unparalleled support to the aviation industry.

Exchange

Our exchange and loan services empower airlines to swiftly acquire top-tier replacements for costly parts, sidestepping substantial inventory expansions and eliminating concerns about core repairs.

AOG

We solve our customers'  challenges  by providing 24/7 AOG (Aircraft on Ground) services, which ensure prompt resolution of issues through the expertise of our  dedicated professionals.

Repair management

Horizon Mechanics offers comprehensive aircraft repair and maintenance services. Our competencies in high- quality repair management ensure efficient and reliable solutions  for our clients.
