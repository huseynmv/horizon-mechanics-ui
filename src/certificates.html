Welcome to Horizon Mechanics LLC


Your Trusted Partner in Aviation Excellence


We are thrilled to announce that Horizon Mechanics LLC is now an official member of the Aviation Suppliers Association (ASA), solidifying our commitment to excellence within the aviation industry. This milestone emphasizes our dedication to delivering superior quality and reliability across all our services.


As proud ASA members, we collaborate exclusively with internationally recognized manufacturing companies, distributors, and suppliers, ensuring that our clients have access to the finest aviation spare parts available. Our partnerships with esteemed industry leaders guarantee that every component we provide meets the highest industry standards and is accompanied by the necessary certifications, offering unparalleled authenticity and reliability.


At Horizon Mechanics LLC, we adhere meticulously to industry regulations, including those established by ASA, EASA, and FAA, placing a premium on exceptional service quality at all times. Our adherence to ISO 9001:2015 certification further underscores our commitment to quality management systems and continual improvement, ensuring consistent delivery of excellence to our valued clients.


In addition to our ASA membership and ISO certification, we are proud to maintain compliance with all regulations and laws within the United Arab Emirates, holding certification from the Dubai Civil Aviation Authority.


Our mission at Horizon Mechanics LLC is to provide maximum value to our clients by offering only the best solutions, backed by unparalleled quality, reliability, and expertise. With our extensive network of trusted partners and stringent quality control measures, we consistently exceed expectations and maintain our position as a leader in the aviation industry.


Join us in our journey towards aviation excellence. Collaborate with Horizon Mechanics LLC today.
