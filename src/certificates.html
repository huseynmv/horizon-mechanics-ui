Our Certificates

Horizon Mechanics LLC is proud to be an official member of the Aviation Suppliers Association (ASA), a demonstration to our commitment to excellence in the aviation industry. As an ASA member, we exclusively collaborate with internationally recognized manufacturing companies, distributors, and suppliers, ensuring that our clients have access to top-quality, highly reliable aviation spare parts.


Horizon Mechanics LLC has established the highest industry standards through strict adherence to ASA, EASA, and FAA regulations, while placing a premium on exceptional service quality. Each component we supply is accompanied by the necessary certifications, guaranteeing its authenticity and reliability.


In addition to our ASA membership, Horizon Mechanics LLC holds ISO 9001:2015 certification, reflecting our unwavering commitment to quality management systems and continuous improvement. This certification ensures that we consistently deliver the highest standards of service and reliability to our clients, providing them with peace of mind and confidence in our offerings.


Furthermore, Horizon Mechanics LLC adheres meticulously to all regulations and laws within the United Arab Emirates and holds certification from the Dubai Civil Aviation Authority.


We are committed to providing maximum value to our clients by offering only the best solutions, backed by unparalleled quality, reliability, and expertise. Our extensive network of trusted partners, coupled with our stringent quality control measures, ensures that we consistently exceed our clients' expectations and maintain our position as a leader in the aviation industry.
